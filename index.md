<div class="profile">

<p>
I am a <strong>Full Professor</strong> of <strong>Distributed Computing</strong> at the Universitat Rovira i Virgili (URV).
</p>

<p>
I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Universitat Pompeu Fabra (UPF), Spain, in 2009.
During my doctoral studies, I worked at
<span class="accent">École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL)</span>, Switzerland.
In the same year, I joined URV.
</p>

<p>
I received the <span class="badge award">Best Paper Award (IEEE LCN 2007)</span>
and the <span class="badge award">Best Dataset Award (ACM IMC 2015)</span>.
I have published more than <strong>90 scientific articles</strong> in IEEE P2P, ACM/IFIP Middleware, IEEE ICDCS, IEEE ICDE, IEEE INFOCOM, and USENIX FAST.
</p>

<p>
Over the years, I have participated in several European research projects:
</p>

<div class="tags">
  <span class="tag">CloudSpaces</span>
  <span class="tag">IOStack</span>
  <span class="tag">CloudButton</span>
  <span class="tag">EXTRACT</span>
  <span class="tag">NEARDATA</span>
  <span class="tag">SIXG</span>
  <span class="tag">CloudStars</span>
</div>

<p>
I also coordinated the Horizon Europe project
<span class="underline">CloudSkin</span> (Grant No. 101092646).
</p>

<p>
I have been involved in several Spanish research projects in Cloud and Edge Computing.
I also contributed to the coordination of
<span class="accent">CloudLess</span>,
under the UNICO R&D Cloud program supporting Spanish companies participating in the IPCEI-CIS initiative.
</p>

<p>
I have served as an evaluator for research and technology transfer projects funded by the
<strong>Agencia Estatal de Investigación (AEI)</strong> and <strong>CDTI</strong>.
</p>

<p>
I have been a visiting researcher at <strong>IBM Research Ireland</strong>.
I have served on Technical Program Committees of IEEE P2P, ACM SYSTOR, IEEE IPDPS, IEEE/ACM CCGrid, IEEE Cloud, and Euro-Par.
I have also served as Guest Editor for <em>Computer Networks (Elsevier)</em>.
</p>

</div>

Furthermore, I have organized several international and national scientific events, including 
<span style="color:#1f77b4; font-weight:600;">IEEE P2P 2012</span> and
